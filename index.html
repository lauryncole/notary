determine what type of notarial act you need!

Four Possible Acts in Oregon:

  1. Acknowledgements (this is for power of attorney moments)
  2. Oaths
  3. Witnessing a Signiture (most common act)
  4. Copy Certifications

bring a copy of the notarial certificate you need (please & thank you).

have your identification documents ready

acceptable forms of identification include:

  • Valid drivers liscense
  • Valid passport
  • US Military ID card (only if it includes a photo)
  • Tribal ID card
  • Other government issued ID if it includes a photo and a signiture
* expired IDs may be accepted if the expiration date is less than 3 years ago

Identification can also be assured if I know you personally OR if you bring along a credible witness that has their own identification documents as listed above.

final checks

Make sure to bring the full and complete document(s) you need notarized. DO NOT bring them signed in advance of the notary appointment.

I cannot provide legal advice or counsel- the scope of a notarization is limited to the four acts listed above.
